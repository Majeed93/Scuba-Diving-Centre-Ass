WELLCOME!!

Welcome to Under Water, located in fabulous Las Vegas, Nevada. We are your Scuba Diving Headquarters for scuba gear and much more. We have sold scuba gear and diving supplies to thousands of Scuba Divers, Water Sports Enthusiasts and Snorkeling Skin Divers just like you.

We have also sold gear to a number of high profile clients such as the Mandalay Bay Hotel Shark Reef Aquarium, Universal Studios, The Bellagio Front Feature Fountain Show, The Bellagio "O" Show, Ripley's Aquarium, The Metropolitan Opera, and even local celebrities.
